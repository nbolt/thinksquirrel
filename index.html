What is Thinksquirrel?

It's your world, We make it work.

At Thinksquirrel, our main mission is equipping developers with powerful tools for making visually realistic games and applications. To do so, we create physics-based products for 2D and 3D projects, integrating closely with game engines and development platforms. Our flagship product, Fluvio, integrates the most realistic fluid dynamics available into projects created in Unity.
